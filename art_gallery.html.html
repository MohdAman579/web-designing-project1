The phrase "art is everywhere" highlights that artistic expression and beauty can be found not just in traditional galleries, but also in everyday objects, nature, and human creations, from the design of a soup can or a cereal box to the music we listen to and the stories we share. This concept encourages a different way of seeing the world, where ordinary things can be viewed as special and art can be a part of everyone's experience, fostering creativity and empathy.
